Why title testing matters for ecommerce categories

Category pages often drive a large share of organic traffic. Small tweaks to the title tag can unlock higher click-through rates, especially for intent-driven keywords where users are comparing options.

  • Highlight differentiators like free shipping or fast delivery.
  • Use keyword variants that match customer language without stuffing.
  • Keep length in check so the snippet renders cleanly on mobile and desktop.

Test setup

Create two variants in Snippet Tester and schedule them so only one runs at a time. Use a 50/50 split across URLs in the category to avoid cannibalizing traffic.

Tip: Pin high-volume URLs to ensure they receive traffic in both variants before declaring a winner.

Reading the results

Monitor the CTR delta in the reporting tab. Look for consistent uplift across your top queries, not just aggregate clicks. If Variant B wins, roll it out in your CMS and schedule a follow-up test for meta descriptions.

Inputs

  • 10–20 category URLs
  • Primary keyword variations
  • Hypothesis notes to share with stakeholders

Outputs

  • CTR delta vs. control
  • Query-level insights
  • Rollout checklist for engineering

What to tweak next

After finalizing the title tag, use the same framework to test meta descriptions, FAQ snippets, and seasonal copy. Document wins so you can reuse them across sibling categories.
